About Lil Bloomers

For over a decade, Lil Bloomers has been a trusted name in North Vancouver. We offer a holistic approach that focuses on essential skills while encouraging creativity, social interaction, and a love for learning.

Our new chapter

In 2025, Lil Bloomers welcomed new ownership. Our team brings deep experience from successfully managing highly regarded daycares across Metro Vancouver — combining community spirit with strong operational standards so families can count on consistent, high-quality care.

Our environment

We recently completed a full interior renovation for brighter, safer, more engaging classrooms. Our outdoor play space is being transformed into a natural playground, and we are proud to offer a full-size indoor gym so children always have room to move — whatever the weather.

Our programs

Age-specific care for every stage — from our youngest blooms to school-age explorers.

Infants & toddlers

0–36 months

A safe, loving, sensory-rich environment for our youngest children, guided by caring specialists who understand early development.

Preschool

3–5 years

A dynamic program that builds curiosity, social skills, and kindergarten readiness through purposeful play, literacy, and numeracy experiences.

Before & after school

Kindergarten – Grade 5

A supportive, fun program with transportation to and from local North Vancouver schools.

We also offer engaging experiences across the week — including storytelling, music with a specialist, art, outdoor play, and more.

Why families choose Lil Bloomers

More than a joyful place — a dedicated team and a thoughtfully designed learning environment.

  • Holistic growth

    We nurture emotional, social, cognitive, and physical development so every child feels seen and supported.

  • Passionate educators

    Our qualified team brings diverse experience and a shared commitment to safety, inclusion, and joyful learning.

  • Strong supervision

    Thoughtful ratios and attentive care help children explore confidently while building trust with caring adults.

  • Partnership with parents

    Open communication and regular updates keep you connected to your child’s day and developmental journey.

How children grow here

Play-based learning sparks curiosity, creativity, and confidence — with rich experiences across the day.

Emotional development

Nurturing empathy, resilience, and self-confidence so children feel secure expressing themselves.

Cognitive development

Literacy, numeracy, problem-solving, and exploration that meet children where they are and stretch their thinking.

Physical development

Active play indoors and out — building strength, balance, coordination, and joyful movement every day.

Social & language

Cooperation, conversation, storytelling, and a language-rich environment that supports friendships and communication.
