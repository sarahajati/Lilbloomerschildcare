Literacy and numeracy without pressure

Pressure-based academics can backfire before age five. Our approach to preschool in North Vancouver, BC emphasizes language-rich conversations, phonological awareness games, rhymes, and books children choose themselves alongside guided read-alouds. For numeracy, we build number sense through cooking projects, sorting collections, block towers, and measurement in the sand table — concrete experiences that stick.

If your child enters preschool in North Vancouver with big feelings or big energy, we focus on co-regulation and predictable routines first.
