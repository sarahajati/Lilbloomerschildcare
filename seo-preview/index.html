FAQ — questions parents ask Google

What age groups do you accept?

We serve infants and toddlers (0–36 months), preschool children (3–5), and school-age students in kindergarten through Grade 5 with before- and after-school care and transportation to local North Vancouver schools.

What is your teacher-to-child ratio?

Ratios follow British Columbia licensing rules for each age group. During your tour we explain how staff are assigned per room and how we maintain supervision indoors, outdoors, and in the gym.

What safety measures do you follow?

We operate as a licensed facility with regular inspections, secure access, allergy-aware meal practices, documented incident procedures, and ongoing staff training. Our recent interior renovation improved sightlines and flow between learning areas.

Fees depend on age group, schedule, and current provincial childcare funding programs. See our childcare cost guide for a detailed discussion, then call 604-987-7673 for current availability and a personalized quote.

Do you offer a full-day program?

Yes — our North Vancouver centre offers full-day early learning and care aligned with working families’ schedules. Ask about opening and closing times when you book a tour.
